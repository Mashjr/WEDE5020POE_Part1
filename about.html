About us

With years of expertise offering a varied and engaging shopping experience in the centre of Central South Africa, Loch Logan Waterfront Mall is a prominent player in the retail sector. Loch Logan Waterfront Mall was established with the goal of being a one-stop shop, entertainment, and cultural destination. Since then, it has developed into a vital community centre.

With a broad tenant mix that ensures every shopper finds what they're searching for, Loch Logan Waterfront Mall has proudly serviced a wide range of customers throughout the years, from local favourites to high-end international brands.

Mission and statements

Excellence

From the retailers they select to the services we offer; they aim for excellence in every facet of our mall.

Inclusivity

Their goal is to establish a friendly, inclusive space that meets the needs of every customer.

Community

Loch Logan Waterfront Mall is committed to being involved and helpful in the neighbourhood, working with groups and projects to have a beneficial influence.
